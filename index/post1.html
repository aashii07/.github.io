Mummy Koala with its babies


I recently had a trip to Australia and GUESS WHAT? I finally saw the Koalas for the first time. The Koala's thick ash-grey fur, white chest and round head with fluffy ears make this species unmistakable. I just could not take my eyes off those cute creatures hugging their mothers. I could not find any other better way than capturing this beautiful moment and share it with you guys! I highly recommend anyone going for a trip to Australia to visit the Cleland Wildlife Park.
IT IS TOTALLY WORTH IT!
