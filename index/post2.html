Best Sushi in Mauritius


The new local restaurant which just opened provides such exquisite sushi. As someone from a completely different culture, I am amazed by how good Japanese cuisine is in our country. I was very amazed by the texture and the softness of this dish consisting of flavourful rice, vinegar and a variety of vegetable. The picture just reflects all that goodness. Mmmm YUMMY!! To be honest, I tried many sushi spots but that of Sushi Park is far the best.
